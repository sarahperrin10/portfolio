Who I am

I am a senior at Duke University from New Jersey, double majoring in English and International Comparative Studies.

This semester, in the Spring of 2019, I took an Interactive Graphics course, my second coding-based class at Duke. I have had so much fun exploring coding in a more creative manner and am proud of the work that I can display here for you! Outside of class, I love the beach, writing and reading, and being with my friends and family.
